# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in IC Copilot a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, experience level, education, socioeconomic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Expected behavior

- Demonstrate empathy and kindness.
- Respect differing research methods and professional experience.
- Give and accept constructive, evidence-based feedback.
- Take responsibility for mistakes and improve the process.
- Protect credentials, licensed data, and private information.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, insults, discriminatory language, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information or credentials without explicit permission.
- Misrepresenting research output, source provenance, or investment performance.
- Sustained disruption of discussions, issues, or reviews.

## Enforcement

Report unacceptable behavior privately to the repository maintainer through GitHub's private vulnerability reporting or another private contact channel listed on the maintainer profile. Reports will be reviewed promptly and handled with discretion.

Maintainers may edit, remove, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that violate this Code of Conduct, and may temporarily or permanently restrict participation.
