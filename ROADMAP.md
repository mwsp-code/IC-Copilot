# Roadmap

IC Copilot prioritizes research integrity, analyst usefulness, and reproducibility over the number of generated ideas.

## v0.1 - Public foundation

- [x] Story-first IC workflow and frozen no-key demos.
- [x] Evidence, causal bridge, valuation, counter-thesis, and monitor trail.
- [x] SEC/XBRL, management sources, macro, peers, consensus adapters, and ADR/FPI profiles.
- [x] Optional guardrailed primary and secondary LLM review.
- [x] Research-integrity benchmark and launch documentation.
- [x] Streamlit, lightweight local UI, CI, Docker, and contributor templates.

## v0.2 - Measured research quality

- [ ] Expand the benchmark to 25 human-labelled historical events.
- [ ] Compare generic LLM, deterministic-only, and guarded-LLM workflows.
- [ ] Publish citation precision, unsupported-claim rate, causal-bridge completeness, and contradiction recall.
- [ ] Add versioned benchmark reports to releases.
- [ ] Add outcome journaling and analyst review labels to demo workflows.

## v0.3 - Company-model depth

- [ ] Auditable three-statement forecast workspace.
- [ ] Segment model templates for major sectors.
- [ ] Debt maturity, refinancing, and credit-spread workspace.
- [ ] Exportable Excel and IC memo packages.
- [ ] Improved historical analogue calibration.

## v0.4 - Global and HKEX expansion

- [ ] First-class HKEX issuer and announcement ingestion.
- [ ] Broader official-source adapters across Europe and Asia.
- [ ] Simplified and Traditional Chinese UI summaries with original-source preservation.
- [ ] Dual-listing liquidity, FX, and share-basis reconciliation.

## Contributor tracks

- Sector and industry playbooks.
- ADR/FPI profiles.
- Metric aliases and derivations.
- Official jurisdiction adapters.
- Benchmark labels and regression fixtures.
- Analyst-facing UX and accessibility.

Roadmap items are proposals, not promises. Open a Discussion for design questions and an Issue when scope and acceptance criteria are clear.
